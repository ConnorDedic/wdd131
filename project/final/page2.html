Termagant

This is a type of Tyranid, an alien race in Warhammer 40K. This is a fun model to paint, as instead of any armor or clothing it has skin and carapace. I used a contrast medium to thin out a pink color to make a weaker color that was able to make a more subtle skin color. I also used edge highlighting on the carapace, as well as detailing the eye on the symbiote weapon.

Otyugh

Otyugh's are an aberration in D&D. They see using eyes on a main tentacle and have two other tentacles that are lined with teeth. I used dry brushing to add depth to the skin.

Gnoll

Gnoll's are another race in D&D. This mini is a good example of how using a few colors with some blending and deviation can add depth to a model.

Morgott the Omen King

Morgott or Margit the Fell Omen is a character in the video game Elden Ring. This is a model that I am presently working on. I 3-d printed the model and it is about 12 inches tall. It had almost 50 pieces and took me over a month to make. I will be practicing a lot of new techniques including making a scheme or effect that looks iridescent.

Skitarii Ranger

This is a Skitarii Ranger from Warhammer: 40k. Warhammer is a grimdark science fantasy tabletop wargame. It was one of the first I made and used a base paint designed to look like martian crust.
